Small Review of them all

1. Harry Potter is just a master piece. A creation of J.K. Rowling, the books are just as good as the movies.

2. Willy Wonka and the Chocolate Factory by Roald Dhal is also great! If you're into funny stories, this novel along with The Glass Elevator is perfect for you.

3. The Silent Patient is a mystery, thriller book. The whole sotry with the plot twist at the end? Alex Michaelides truly made a psychological thriller.

4. The Shatter Me series by Tahereh Mafi is perfect for you if you're into fiction, romance and adventure.

5. And last but not least, The Wimpy Kid Series. Who doesn't know about it? It's the most funniest series ever. Perfect for tweens and teens alike.
